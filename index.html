FAQ

12 clear answers before you decide.

What is RelationshipScan?

RelationshipScan is a structured reflection tool for relationship uncertainty. It helps you organize observations and responses into a clearer view.

How does the scan work?

You answer a short set of questions. The tool then generates your Trust Index, a short interpretation, and an optional path to a deeper report.

How accurate is the result?

The result is a structured estimate based on your inputs in this session. It is useful for clarity, but it is not an absolute or final truth.

Is this a psychological diagnosis?

No. This is not a diagnosis, therapy, or legal judgment. It is an interpretative support tool for clearer thinking.

What do I get for free?

You get your Trust Index, a short interpretation, and selected visible insights. This gives useful direction, but not the full analytical breakdown.

What is included in the full report?

The full report includes detailed section analysis, premium signal interpretation, charts, possible scenarios, and practical next-step guidance.

Why do I need to pay for the full report?

The free layer gives orientation. The paid report adds deeper structure, richer interpretation, and decision-focused detail that takes more analysis.

How long does the scan take?

Most users complete the scan in about 5 minutes. You can do it in one session on mobile or desktop.

Is my data safe?

We use minimal data for service operation. Payment is handled securely by Stripe, and core scan flow data is stored in your browser storage.

Can I use this more than once?

Yes. You can retake the scan to compare patterns over time, especially after meaningful conversations or relationship changes.

Who is this tool for?

It is for adults who want a calm, structured view before making emotional relationship decisions.

What should I do after seeing my result?

Review recurring patterns, separate facts from assumptions, and choose one concrete next step. If needed, use the full report for deeper context.
