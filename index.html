FAQ

12 clear answers before you decide.

What is RelationshipScan?

A short questionnaire that turns what you are living into a Trust Index and a blunt read — then a paid layer if you want the full write-up.

How does the scan work?

You click answers. You get a score band and copy tied to those clicks. If you pay, you get the long version with the same inputs.

How accurate is the result?

Only as honest as your answers in this moment. Useful for decisions, not a court transcript.

Is this a psychological diagnosis?

No. Not therapy. Not legal advice. A paid read on your answers.

What do I get for free?

Trust Index, short read, a few locked sections with enough text to see if the paid part is worth it.

What is included in the full report?

Area-by-area write-up, what is weak, what still holds, where it drifts in a few months, and what to do next without the fluff.

Why do I need to pay for the full report?

Because the free layer is supposed to stop early. The paid chunk is where we stop being polite and spell it out.

How long does the scan take?

Most users complete the scan in about 5 minutes. You can do it in one session on mobile or desktop.

Is my data safe?

We use minimal data for service operation. Payment is handled securely by Stripe, and core scan flow data is stored in your browser storage.

Can I use this more than once?

Yes. Re-run after a fight, a talk, or a month of silence — see if the answers move.

Who is this tool for?

Adults who want a straight read before they text, break up, or pretend it is fine.

What should I do after seeing my result?

Write down what happened three times this week. Pay if you still cannot name the main problem.
